Semester 1 (Fall 2025) — Weeks 1–16

Week 1 (Sept 1–5) — First Breath: Definitions & Tools

Focus: IM U1.L1–L2 • Launch precise definitions & tool fluency; set classroom systems. Day 1 mirrors Algebra 1 welcome flow.

Daily Plan (50 min each)

  • Launch Mon — First Day: Welcome & Norms
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: “What is a definition?” (2 examples, 1 counterexample) → pair share.
    • 5–13 Mini: Course overview, color‑band rhythm, notebook setup (tape‑in covers + table of contents).
    • 14–34 Activity: A1‑style community builder + quick tool tour; pass manila folder & mini‑folders.
    • 34–39 Peer Check: Materials check + expectations.
    • 39–45 Debrief: Why precision matters in geometry.
    • 45–50 Reflection Prompt: “Write a precise definition of ‘segment’ and give a non‑example.”
  • Applied Studio Tue — IM U1.L1: Build It
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: Label & mark a diagram (⟂, ∥, ≅).
    • 5–13 Mini: Compass & straightedge safety; markings convention anchor.
    • 14–34 Activity: L1 Build‑It stations (draw, label, justify choices).
    • 34–39 Color‑Swap Peer Check on accuracy.
    • 39–45 Notebook Insert: “Markings Quick‑Guide” + definition table.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “Which marking did you misuse at first? Fix it with a counterexample.”
  • Synthesis Thu — IM U1.L2: Constructing Patterns
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: Pattern noticing (What stays invariant?).
    • 5–13 Mini: Precision sentence stems (“because…”, “therefore…”).
    • 14–34 Activity: L2 construction prompts → pattern tiles.
    • 34–39 Gallery stroll + 1 sticky question per tile.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in best artifact; annotate steps with verbs.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “Rewrite a classmate’s step set to be more precise.”
  • Assess/Buffer Fri — Skill/Buffer
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up Check: 3 quick ID items (point, segment, ray).
    • 5–20 Mini Skill‑Check S0 (tools & markings) — redoable.
    • 20–40 Constructions practice lab (free choice from L1–L2).
    • 40–50 Notebook Quick‑Flip & Stamps; Reflection: “One definition I’ll defend next week is…”

Notebook Inserts: Definition table • Markings Quick‑Guide • L1/L2 best‑work artifact

Resources: IM G U1.L1–L2; Discovering Geometry openers; All Things Algebra vocab cards

Next Steps: Print bisector step cards; prep GeoGebra file for locus trace; queue Desmos Transformations Golf for Week 4.

Week 2 (Sept 8–12) — Bisectors & Why‑They‑Work

Focus: IM U1.L3–L4 • Perpendicular bisector, angle bisector, equilateral triangle; introduce “because” justifications.

Daily Plan (50 min each)

  • Launch Mon — IM U1.L3: Perpendicular Bisectors
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: Equidistance quick‑sketch (two points → locus guess).
    • 5–13 Mini: Why ⟂ bisector is the equidistance locus.
    • 14–34 Activity: L3 constructions; measure & highlight equal distances.
    • 34–39 Peer Check: “Because…” sentence stems.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in exemplar with 3 justifications.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “Where did your intuition disagree with the result?”
  • Applied Studio Tue — IM U1.L4: Equilateral Triangles
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: Two circles → what triangle forms?
    • 5–13 Mini: Congruent segments & triangle properties.
    • 14–34 Activity: L4 equilateral builds; variant challenges.
    • 34–39 Peer Check for equal radii & marked congruence.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in + annotate construction reasons.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “Write a 2‑sentence proof that your triangle is equilateral.”
  • Synthesis Thu — Studio: Compass Flower (Art Integration)
    • 0–4 Warm‑Up: Invariance in repeating distances.
    • 5–13 Mini: From bisectors/equilateral to design constraints.
    • 14–39 Activity: Compass Flower tile; emphasize clean construction marks.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in photo or paper artifact.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “Name the constructions hiding in your design.”
  • Assess/Buffer Fri — Skill Check S1 + Practice
    • 0–10 Warm‑Up Review: L3/L4 quick IDs.
    • 10–25 Skill Check S1 (bisectors & equilateral) — redoable.
    • 25–45 Targeted practice (A.T. Algebra sheets); optional extensions.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “One precise step I’ll never skip again is…”

Notebook Inserts: Bisector step cards • Equilateral construction steps • “Because” stem bank

Week 3 (Sept 15–19) — ∥/⟂ Through a Point; Inscribed Square

Focus: IM U1.L5–L6–L7 • Perpendicular/angle bisectors through a point; parallel lines; inscribed square construction.

    • 5–13 Mini: Angle bisector justification via symmetry.

    • 34–39 Peer Check on correctness of intersections.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in + margin proof sentence.
    • 45–50 Reflection: “What would break this construction?”

    • 5–13 Mini: Parallel via equal corresponding angles.
    • 14–34 Activity: L6 constructions; quick ∥ checker.
    • 34–39 Peer Check: angle mark consistency.
    • 39–45 Tape‑in + 2 justifications.

  • Synthesis Thu — IM U1.L7: Squares (Inscribed & Given Side)

    • 5–13 Mini: Two constructions; pitfalls.
    • 14–39 Activity: L7 builds; choose one to perfect for tape‑in.
    • 39–45 Notebook: Annotate constraints & right‑angle evidence.

    • 0–10 Warm‑Up: Wild Wonders Midpoint (mini) — connect to loci.
    • 10–40 Choice Studio: refine L5–L7 artifacts or extension challenges.
    • 40–50 Reflection: “Map a real object that could be built from these constructions.”
